02 — Magazine Covers

Print thinking
in a digital world

B&W Editorial
A bold experiment in monochrome editorial design. Stripping colour forces a sharper focus on typography, whitespace, and structural tension — the foundations that outlast any trend.

(Devesh, a designer, sits at his desk staring at a blank screen with "Magazine Cover Design" written on it.)

"How can someone not experiment with a black-and-white magazine cover page?"

(The experiment continues for hours, but the designer has decided not to take reference from anywhere.)

"I don't feel like it's my zone. Whatever — let's find out."

(Hours later — two covers exist where there were none.)
